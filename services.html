Our Services

Gathering Centre for Hire

Host your events in our spacious gathering centre, equipped with all necessary amenities to make your event a success. Whether it's a wedding, conference, or community event, we have the space for you.

Kitchen Gardening Demonstrations

Join our workshops to learn sustainable gardening practices that you can implement at home. Our experts will guide you through the process of creating your own kitchen garden.

Environmental Protection Initiatives

Participate in our tree planting and restoration efforts to help keep our environment green and healthy. Get involved in community clean-up days and learn about conservation.

Skilling Programs

Enhance your skills in various trades such as motorcycle mechanics, carpentry, welding, hairdressing, and tailoring. Our programs are designed to empower individuals with practical skills for employment.
